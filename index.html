About Nature Nest Global

Bridging India's agricultural heritage
with the world's finest tables.

Nature Nest Global is a premium Indian food exporter dedicated to sourcing nature's most nourishing ingredients — directly from certified farms and processing centres across India — and delivering them with uncompromising quality to retailers, distributors and wholesalers across five continents.

Every shipment is built on three commitments: pure ingredients, audited supply chains, and partnerships that last.

Foxnuts (Makhana)

Global Supply Capability

One partner. From farm to FOB.

We operate the full export chain in-house — sourcing, processing, packing, documentation and shipping — so our buyers get a single, accountable partner across geographies.

01

Sourcing & Lab Testing

Direct procurement from certified farms; lab-tested for moisture, contaminants, pesticide residues and origin authenticity.

02

Premium Processing

Sortex cleaning, grading and food-safe processing in HACCP-compliant facilities. Optional steam sterilisation.

03

Custom Packaging

Multi-layer, food-grade packaging in retail SKUs and bulk formats. Private-label & multilingual artwork supported.

04

Export Documentation

Phytosanitary, COA, COO, fumigation and customs paperwork — fully managed by our in-house export desk.

05

Shipping & Incoterms

FOB, CIF, CFR & DDP shipping via India's major ports — Mundra, Nhava Sheva and Chennai.

06

Last-mile Coordination

Door-to-door delivery support, container tracking and dedicated point-of-contact through to receipt.
